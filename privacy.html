Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 9, 2026

Yochy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Yochy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data. By using Yochy you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile: name, email address, date of birth, gender, profile photos, bio, and interests you provide.
  • Authentication: hashed passwords and OAuth tokens (e.g. Google Sign-In).
  • Messages & Content: messages, photos, videos, and any content you send, receive, or post in the App.
  • Location: approximate or precise location when you enable location-based matching features.
  • Device & Usage: device identifiers, OS version, app version, IP address, crash reports, and analytics about how you use the App.
  • Payment Metadata: purchase tokens and transaction IDs processed via Google Play Billing. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Push Tokens: FCM/APNS tokens required to send you push notifications.
  • AI Interactions: prompts and outputs related to AI-powered features when you use them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide matching, messaging, media, and all in-app features.
  • Process and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Personalize recommendations and content.
  • Send account, security, and service notifications.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and safety risks.
  • Analyze usage to improve and debug the App.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis (EU / EEA Users)

Where required by law, we process your data on the basis of: performance of a contract (providing the service); consent (optional features); legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, improvements); and legal compliance.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: hosting, analytics, push notifications, and payment processors that act on our behalf and are bound by data-protection agreements.
  • Other Users: profile information you choose to display is visible to other users of the App as designed.
  • Legal Requests: we may disclose data to comply with valid legal processes or to protect safety and rights.
  • Business Transfers: in a merger or acquisition, data may transfer to the successor entity.
  • We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. On account deletion we delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for fraud prevention.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. We apply access controls and industry-standard safeguards. No method of transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights:

  • Use in-app account settings (Settings → Account → Delete Account) to delete your account and data.
  • Email privacy@yochy.app for any other data request.
  • See our Data Deletion page for full instructions.

8. Children's Privacy

Yochy is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We take appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) when transferring data internationally.

10. California Residents (CCPA)

California residents may request disclosure of personal information we have collected, shared, or sold in the past 12 months, and may request deletion. Contact us at privacy@yochy.app. We do not sell personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will post the updated version with a revised date. Significant changes will be communicated via the App or email.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@yochy.app
