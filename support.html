Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app, or see our Data Deletion page for email instructions.

Why was my Google Sign-In rejected?

Make sure you're using the same Google account you registered with. If the issue persists, email support@yochy.app with your registered email address.

I was charged but didn't receive Premium.

Restore your purchase by going to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, email support@yochy.app with your Google Play order ID.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Google Play. Open Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Yochy Premium → Cancel subscription. You retain Premium access until the end of the current daily billing period.
