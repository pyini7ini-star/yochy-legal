Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 9, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of your Yochy account and all associated personal data. This page explains exactly what gets deleted and how to submit a deletion request.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the App (instant)

Step 1

Open the Yochy app and go to Profile → Settings → Account.

Step 2

Tap "Delete Account" at the bottom of the Account screen.

Step 3

Confirm by entering your email address and tapping "Permanently Delete".

Your account is scheduled for deletion immediately. Personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email deletion request

If you can no longer access the app, send an email from the address associated with your Yochy account to:

privacy@yochy.app

Subject line: Account Deletion Request
Include: the email address registered to your account. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation reply.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, bio, photos, interests, preferences)
  • Your matches and conversations
  • Messages sent and received
  • Location data
  • Analytics and activity logs associated with your account
  • Push notification tokens
  • OAuth connections (e.g. Google Sign-In link)

What is retained

  • Transaction records required for tax and legal compliance (retained per applicable law, typically 7 years, stored anonymized)
  • Content that was reported for abuse or safety purposes, retained only as long as necessary to investigate or comply with law enforcement requests
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics that cannot identify you
Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Active subscriptions via Google Play must be cancelled separately through Google Play → Subscriptions to stop future billing.

Cancel a Google Play subscription

  • Open Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  • Find Yochy Premium and tap Cancel subscription
  • Cancelling stops future billing. Deleting your Yochy account does not automatically cancel an active Play Store subscription.

Data portability

Before deleting, you may request a copy of your personal data. Email privacy@yochy.app with subject "Data Export Request". We will provide a machine-readable export within 30 days.
